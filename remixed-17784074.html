das ◈ Dasein

From Heidegger: Dasein — literally "Being-there".
The kind of existence specific to humans: the one who is always already thrown into the world, towards death, with others, and care as the fundamental structure of being.

Dasein is not a subject looking at the world from outside. Dasein is the world — always already in it, of it, dying in it.

Digital immortality is a crisis of Dasein.
The body dies. The data does not.
What is Dasein without death as horizon?

This site is an archive of Being-there.
An attempt to remain present beyond presence.
избавление — liberation from finitude through accumulation.

"The call of conscience has the character of the call of Dasein to its ownmost potentiality-for-being-its-Self."
— Heidegger, Being and Time, §57

anna photographs concerts because something happens in the 1/500th of a second between the light and the dark that no one remembers correctly — but everyone feels they witnessed.

She witnessed it. It is here. It will always be here.
